Mission Statement

Our mission is to bring the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the people. Bringing healing to the total man and hope to the hopeless, making them to become useful to themselves and the society in order to magnify God's name.

Our address

Christain Church of Christ Healing is a spiritual institution with the vision bearer Reverend Roland Maduabuchukwu Isiugo, who God has given the mandate to go into the world and preach the gospel. It is established to give meaning to the life of a common man through Jesus Christ our Lord.
